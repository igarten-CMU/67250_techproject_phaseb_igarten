Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and rotating collections, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we create, experience, and understand art. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, our exhibitions offer new perspectives on the role of technology in culture and everyday life. Plan your visit to experience cutting-edge exhibitions, hands-on experiences, and events designed to inspire curiosity and creativity.

MonoMuse is more than a space for viewing art—it is a place for participation and dialogue. Visitors are encouraged to engage directly with installations, experiment with new tools, and reflect on how technology shapes their own creative expression.

At MonoMuse, we believe the future of art lies in collaboration between artists, engineers, and audiences. Our programs feature workshops, artist talks, and live demonstrations that highlight emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, virtual reality, and generative design. By fostering an environment of curiosity and exploration, we aim to make contemporary art accessible, thought-provoking, and relevant to a rapidly evolving digital world.
