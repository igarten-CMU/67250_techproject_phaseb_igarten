Exhibtion 1: Neon Dreams: The Art of Light and Code

This exhibition explores the intersection of neon art and digital technology, showcasing mesmerizing installations that use LED lights and algorithms to create dynamic, ever-evolving visual experiences. Artists challenge traditional light forms by integrating programming and interactive elements, allowing visitors to engage with the artwork in real time.

Exhibition 2: Virtual Realities: The Evolution of Digital Worlds

"Virtual Realities" takes visitors through the history of virtual environments, from early digital landscapes to fully immersive VR experiences. This exhibition highlights the creative potential of digital spaces, with interactive displays that invite visitors to step into alternate realities crafted by groundbreaking artists and technologists.
